Earthquake Data

An earthquake is caused by a sudden slip on a fault. The tectonic plates are always slowly moving, but they get stuck at their edges due to friction. When the stress on the edge overcomes the friction, there is an earthquake that releases energy in waves that travel through the earth's crust and cause the shaking that we feel.These Earthquakes's datas are real time datas from United States Geological Survey (USGS) using Leaflet for ploting. Magnitude of each earhtquake is represented by the circles present on the map.
